Resume

2011-2014

After graduating from college, I began working at East View Geospatial in Minneapolis as a cartographer. I worked on many large-scale map and data production projects while adhering to strict geographic standards. I became a lead member of a team of 15 professionals and helped implement standards and procedures to improve efficiency.

2014-2015

After 3 years of working in a data production capacity, I wanted to experience other ways GIS is applied. I began working for a pipeline engineering and consulting firm called Lake Superior Consulting. I was a GIS analyst contracted to Enbridge Energy as geospatial support on multiple pipeline construction projects. Along with my duties of map and map book creation, assuring data integrity, and spatial analysis, I also taught CAD technicians the basics of using ArcGIS to improve CAD/GIS integration on multiple projects.

2015-present

I currently enjoy employment as a GIS analyst with Hennepin County, Minnesota. I work on a wide range of projects from road reconstruction designs, and bike route mapping, to pedestrian counts and helping with GIS training opportunities. One of my major attractions to public sector employment is the ability to share what I do with others. My work helps inform decisions made at the county level that, hopefully, improve the lives of citizens of Hennepin County.
